Frequently-Asked Questions

How can I get Printing Quote and what specification do you require?

You may contact us here with the following details;


- Quantity to be printed

- Number of pages (for books only)

- Specify the colours for text printing and cover printing (for books only)

- Specify the trim size

- Specify the materia

How do I submit my artwork?

All artwork designs can be submitted in the following format;


 - Adobe Illustrator (AI)

- Adobe Acrobat Document (PDF)

- In Design

How will my job be delivered back to me?

If you are a customer out of the Peninsular Malaysia your job will be delivered to through sea or air shipment depending upon the value and urgency of your requirement and if you are a customer residing in the Peninsular Malaysia then your goods will be delivered by lorry.

How can I be assured of a high quality job without a press check?

We provide a complete set of digital proofs for all jobs for your confirmation and upon your confirmation then only our printing process will commence.

How long does it take to process my order?

After confirmation of artwork, it requires 7 to 21 working days depending on your product specification.

Who should I contact about the status of my job?

Please contact us using Contact link.

What are the payment terms and how to make payment?

The usual payment terms are 50% deposit upon confirmation and 50% balance upon completion otherwise stated. Payments could be made by cash, cheque or interbank fund transfer and upon making the payment please fax or email us the slip.
